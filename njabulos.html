About Njabulo's Kitchen

Njabulo's Kitchen brings fresh South African home-cooked meals straight to your door. Founded in 2024 by Njabulo Malala, our mission is to share the taste of home with busy professionals, students, and anyone who loves good food.

We specialize in traditional and fast-food dishes, and our famous chakalaka and fresh spicies. All meals are prepared with love and the freshest ingredients sourced from local markets.

Our Services

  • Daily meal deliveries (order by 10am for same-day delivery)
  • Weekly meal plans with discounted rates
  • Catering for events and special occasions
  • Cooking classes
