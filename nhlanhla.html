About Nhlanhla Properties

Urban Haven Rentals offers stylish, fully-furnished modern homes designed for comfort, convenience, and lifestyle. Founded in 2021 by Sibusiso Nhlanhla, our mission is to make finding your perfect home effortless, whether you’re staying for a weekend, a month, or longer..

We specialize in contemporary apartments and houses with premium finishes, smart-home features, and prime locations. Every property is maintained to the highest standard and thoughtfully equipped so you can settle in immediately..

Our Services

  • Short-term stays - Perfect for vacations, business trips, or temporary relocations.
  • Long-term rentals - Flexible lease options for professionals, students, and families.
  • Fully-furnished & move-in ready - Modern décor, high-speed Wi-Fi, and essential utilities included.
  • Property hosting & management - For homeowners looking to rent out their spaces hassle-free.
